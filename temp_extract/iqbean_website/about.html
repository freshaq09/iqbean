Our Story

IQBEAN was founded in 2018 after our founders experienced the frustration of traditional recruiting methods while building technical teams. They knew there had to be a better way to connect companies with IT talent that truly met their needs.

Starting with a small network of vetted developers in the Chicago area, IQBEAN quickly expanded nationally as clients experienced the difference our precision matching approach made to their projects and teams.

Today, we work with companies ranging from Fortune 500 enterprises to innovative startups, providing IT staffing solutions that focus on quality matches rather than volume of resumes. Our team has grown to include specialists across all major technology domains, ensuring we can support the full spectrum of IT staffing needs.
