Our Placement Process

We've designed a streamlined, transparent process to connect you with opportunities that match your skills and career goals.

1

Join Our Network

Submit your profile through our talent network form. We'll review your experience, technical skills, and career goals to understand what opportunities would be the best match for you.

2

Initial Consultation

Connect with one of our specialized technical recruiters who understands your skill domain. We'll discuss your background, preferences, and the types of opportunities you're interested in exploring.

3

Opportunity Matching

We'll present you with opportunities that align with your skills, experience, and career goals. You'll receive detailed information about the role, company, team, and technical environment to help you make informed decisions.

4

Interview Preparation

For opportunities you're interested in pursuing, we'll provide comprehensive interview preparation including company insights, technical assessment guidance, and interview coaching to help you present your best self.

5

Ongoing Support

After placement, we maintain contact to ensure your transition into the new role is smooth and successful. We're your advocate throughout the entire process and beyond.
