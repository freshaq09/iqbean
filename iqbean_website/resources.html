Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our IT staffing and recruitment services.

How long does it typically take to fill an IT position?

Our average time-to-fill for IT roles is 2-3 weeks for contract positions and 3-5 weeks for direct hires. However, this can vary based on the specificity of the role, seniority level, and market conditions. For urgent needs, we offer expedited services that can identify qualified candidates within days.

What makes IQBEAN different from other IT staffing firms?

IQBEAN distinguishes itself through our deep technical expertise (all our recruiters have IT backgrounds), our proprietary technical assessment framework, and our focus on cultural alignment. We don't just match keywords on resumes – we comprehensively evaluate technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and cultural fit.

Do you offer guarantees on your placements?

Yes, all our direct hire placements come with a satisfaction guarantee. If a placed candidate leaves within 90 days, we'll find a replacement at no additional cost. For contract placements, we offer a 5-day guarantee period to ensure the professional meets your expectations.

What geographic areas do you serve?

IQBEAN operates nationally across the United States with specialized focus in major tech hubs including San Francisco, Seattle, Austin, New York, Boston, and Chicago. We also support remote placements and have international capabilities through our partner network in Europe, Canada, and Asia-Pacific.

How do you screen candidates for technical skills?

Our screening process includes a multi-layered technical evaluation: resume review, initial technical phone screening, role-specific technical assessment (coding challenges, system design exercises, etc.), and technical interview with a subject matter expert in the candidate's specialization. We can customize this process to align with your specific requirements.

What are your payment terms and fee structure?

For direct hires, our fees are typically a percentage of the candidate's first-year base salary, paid upon successful placement. For contract roles, we charge a markup on the hourly rate. We offer flexible payment terms and volume discounts for companies with ongoing staffing needs. Contact us for a customized quote.
